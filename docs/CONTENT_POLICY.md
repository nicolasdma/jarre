# Content Policy

Jarre is open source for code, but educational content and media can carry separate rights.

## Contributor Requirements

When contributing content/assets, you must ensure at least one applies:

- You created the material.
- The material is in the public domain.
- The material license permits redistribution and derivative use.
- You have explicit permission to redistribute.

## Prohibited Contributions

Do not commit:

- copyrighted book text/images without redistribution rights
- paid-course assets without permission
- private notes or proprietary datasets from third parties

## Attribution

When a contribution includes licensed third-party material, include:

- source link
- license type
- required attribution text

## Review Enforcement

Maintainers may request evidence of licensing or remove contributions with unclear rights.
